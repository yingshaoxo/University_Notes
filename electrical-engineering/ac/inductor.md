$$
\begin{align*}
i &= \sqrt{2} I \sin(wt + \psi_i)
\\ \\ \\
u =  L\frac{di}{dt} = L \cdot i^\prime &= \sqrt{2} LIw \cos(wt + \psi_i) = \sqrt{2}LIw \sin(wt + \psi_i + \frac{\pi}{2})

u &= \sqrt{2} U \sin(wt + \psi_i + \frac{\pi}{2}) = \sqrt{2} U \sin(wt + \psi_u)
\end{align*}
$$

$$
