Anti-derivative
导数反向
___

Antiderivative is the reverse relation of derivative. 

For example, we know that the derivative of $$x^2$$ is $$2x$$. This means that an antiderivative of $$2x$$ is $$x^2$$.
___

$$\text{Indefinite integration} = \text{Anti-derivative}$$
___
