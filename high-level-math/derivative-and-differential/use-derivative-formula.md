##### For composition of functions

$$
\begin{align*}
y &= e^{-x}
\\ \\
y^\prime &= e^{-x} \times (-x)^\prime
\\ \\    &= -e^{-x}
\end{align*}
$$


##### For implicit function or relation

$$
x^2 + y^2 = 0
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
\\
&2x + 2yy^\prime = 0
\\ \\
&y^\prime = \frac{-2x}{2y}
\end{align*}
$$
